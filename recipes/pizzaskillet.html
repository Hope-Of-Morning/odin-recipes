How to make a High Protein, Low Carb Pizza Skillet

Description

If you’re looking for delicious high protein low carb recipes for weight loss, then you have to try this pizza skillet. It’s a great way to indulge your pizza cravings without derailing your diet. Customize with your favorite pizza toppings and dig into a big, hot dish of comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small sweet onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 large zucchini, diced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 6 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 12 pepperoni
  • dried oregano, to garnish
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet. Drain on a plate lined with paper towels, and wipe out the excess grease from the pan.
  2. Preheat the oven to broil.
  3. Add the olive oil, onion, bell pepper, zucchini, and a generous pinch of salt and pepper. Sauté for about 7-8 minutes or until the vegetables are soft and fragrant
  4. Add the cooked ground beef and marinara sauce. Stir until everything is combined and spread evenly
  5. Sprinkle the cheese evenly over the top, followed by scattering the pepperoni out evenly.
  6. Place into the oven and broil for 5-7 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the pepperonis have crisped up slightly.
  7. Garnish with dried oregano if desired and enjoy!
